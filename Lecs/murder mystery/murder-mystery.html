Scene of the Crime: The Dining Room

At precisely 10:16 PM, a scream shattered the silence of Ravenwood Manor. Minutes later, the lifeless body of Lord Reginald Blackwood was found slumped over the dining table, a glass of untouched wine in hand. The chandelier flickered. The scent of lavender lingered in the air—mixed now with something more sinister.

There were no signs of forced entry. The windows were locked from the inside. Every guest in the manor had a motive—inheritance, revenge, secrets. A torn letter was discovered in the fireplace, still warm to the touch. The grandfather clock had mysteriously stopped at 10:16. And somewhere in the shadows, the real killer still lurked...
